Online Notepad

Privacy Policy

At Online Notepad (blocodenotasonline.com.br), we respect your privacy and are committed to handling your data securely and transparently. Our tool allows you to create and save notes and task lists both locally in your browser and in the cloud using Firebase. Below, we detail how your data is processed, especially regarding registration, login, and Firebase as a database.

Data Collection

Without Login: If you choose to use Online Notepad without logging in, your notes and task lists will continue to be saved locally in your browser using local storage. In this case, no personal data or notes are sent to our servers.

With Login: If you choose to create an account or log in, we collect only the data necessary for authentication and account usage, such as your email address. Your notes and task lists will be saved in Firebase, a cloud database platform that enables synchronization across different devices. This ensures that your notes are accessible from anywhere as long as you log in.

Security

Local Storage: When using Online Notepad without login, your notes remain stored locally in your browser. To ensure the security of this information, we recommend protecting access to your device and browser.

Firebase: If you choose to use the login feature, your notes and task lists will be stored in Firebase. Firebase employs advanced security measures to protect stored data, including encryption and authentication. Additionally, we only use the data strictly necessary to ensure the app's functionality, such as your registered email.

Use of Cookies

Our site does not use cookies to track your activities or collect personal information. However, when using Firebase for authentication and data storage, session cookies may be used to maintain your login while navigating the site. These cookies are essential for the service to function and are not used for marketing or advertising purposes.

Data Sharing

Without Login: If you are not logged in, your notes remain on your device and are not shared with us or third parties.

With Login: Data saved in Firebase, such as your notes and lists, are protected and not shared with third parties, except when required by law. We use Firebase solely for storing and synchronizing your data, ensuring your privacy.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information stored in Firebase. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, please contact us, and we will take the necessary actions.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically to reflect changes in our services or applicable regulations. Any changes will be posted on this page. We recommend reviewing this policy regularly to stay informed about any modifications.
